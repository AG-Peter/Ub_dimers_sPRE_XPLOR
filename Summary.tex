    linear combination was built as such:

    \begin{align}
    \mathbf{sPRE} &= v_m &= x1 \cdot v_{c1} + x2 \cdot v_{c2} \hdots \\
    \mathbf{sPRE} &= y &= \begin{bmatrix}
           v_(m, a) \\
            \\
           \vdots \\
           x_{m}
         \end{bmatrix}
    (sPRE_1, sPRE_2, ...) &= x1 * (sPRE_1, sPRE_2, sPRE_3), x2 * (sPRE_1, sPRE_2, ...)
    \end{align}
